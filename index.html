詠春 · Ving Tsun

The Ancient Training Tool

The Muk Yan Jong — "wooden dummy" or "plum blossom dummy" — is a cornerstone of Ving Tsun Kung Fu. Masters have trained against its arms and leg for centuries.

The jong develops precision, timing, structure, and power. It trains the practitioner to move around a fixed opponent — applying the 108-movement form that represents the full vocabulary of Ving Tsun's close-range combat system.

Unlike free-weight or bag training, the jong gives back. It teaches angles, deflection, simultaneous attack and defense, and the economy of motion that defines Ving Tsun.

The Living Tradition

A Lineage That Matters

Every jong we build carries an unbroken chain of knowledge stretching from the original Ving Tsun masters to the training floor of Denver Kung Fu today.

Origin · Southern China

Yim Ving Tsun

Founder of the system. The art is named in her honor — a martial art designed to be efficient, scientific, and accessible to anyone.

Hong Kong · 1949–1972

Grandmaster Yip Man (Ip Man)

Brought Ving Tsun to the public. Teacher of Bruce Lee. Produced the most revered masters of the modern era, including Moy Yat, Wong Shun Leung, and Chu Shong Tin.

New York City · 1973

Grandmaster Moy Yat

Became a fully sanctioned Sifu under Yip Man at 24. Moved to America in 1973. An accomplished artist, acupuncturist, and master of masters — he founded the Special Student Association, whose members are the Grandmasters teaching Ving Tsun worldwide today.

Richmond, VA · 1986

Grandmaster Moy Tung

Began training under Moy Yat in 1980. Became a sanctioned Sifu in 1986. Has devoted his life to the authentic transmission of Ving Tsun across the United States. At his school's 30-year anniversary, over 200 masters and Sifus gathered from around the world.

Iowa City · Virginia Beach

Sifu Moy Yat Tung

Senior student of Moy Tung. Surgeon, father, guitarist. His schools have produced some of the most powerful Sifus currently teaching, with over a dozen schools carrying his lineage.

Denver, Colorado · Est. 1999

Sifu Ely Matson · Denver Kung Fu

Member of the MY4 and ICC inner circles. Close grandstudent of Moy Tung and student of Sifu Moy Yat Tung. Training in Ving Tsun since 1999. Founder of Denver Kung Fu.
